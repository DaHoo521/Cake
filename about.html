About Us

Founded in 2009, Cups 'N' Cakes combines traditional American baking with modern flavors. Our mission is to bring joy to every celebration, big or small.

From our humble beginnings in Las Vegas to our new home in Scotland, we remain committed to quality, creativity, and community.
