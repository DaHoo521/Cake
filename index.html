Welcome to Cups N Cakes

Generations of Baking inspired with a Modern Twist


Cups 'N' Cakes by Monica is a True American Bakehouse. The owner Monica Finch-Robertson relocated from Las Vegas, Nevada to Scotland in 2009. Cups 'N' Cakes built it's reputation on baking the Best brownies, blondies, and true American style Whoopie Pies in town. We also specialise in custom cupcakes, celebration cakes, traditional American Apple and Pecan pies. Need an event catered? Let us do the work for you, we can provide a fantastic sweet and savoury buffet for you and your guests.
